Our Mission

Women in Science strives to support and encourage our female students in their pursuit of the sciences. We have officers, members and alumni who have lots of advice to share. We plan events that encourage women to pursue a career in the sciences. Collaborating with USF faculty and students within the sciences, Women in Science is a great organization for all students and can complement the educational mission of USF.
